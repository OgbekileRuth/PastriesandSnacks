Samosa ingredients

  • 150g of all purpose flour
  • 200g of minced meat
  • 1 handful of green peas
  • 2 medium size onion
  • 3 medium size irish potatoes
  • Salt, water, curry, thyme, Red dry pepper
  • 1 season cube and Vegetable oil

Step-by-step instruction

  • Wash and diced the irish potatoes, carrots and onions into different plates and set aside.
  • Boil the minced meats. Add curry, thyme, 1 seasoned cube and a pinch of salt to taste and set a side.
  • Boil the diced potatoes, carrots, green peas and onions with the stock meats till the carrots is soft.Then drain the water out.
  • Fry the minced meat with the vegetable oil,add curry, thyme and red dry pepper and mix properly.
  • Add the drained potatoes, carrots, greenpeas, onions and mix properly and set aside.

    • Samosa dough cooking direction

    • Pour the flour, add vegetable oil, add a pinch of salt and a little warm water bit by bit and mix properly until you get stretchy dough.
    • Cover the samosa dough for like 25 minutes with an air tight aluminium foil. then knead the dough to make it more elastic.
    • Place the dough on a well floured surface. Roll out dough about 2 inch thick. using a glass cup or a small round bowl of about 4 inches cut out circles by gently pressing on the dough and releasing it.
    • Cut each into two 2 equal halves. Form a cone shape with one of the halves. Scoop some meat sauce into the cone shape and cover it.
    • Do this for the remaining samosa and arrange it in a tray. Put some vegetable oil into a deep frying pan and fry until it is golden brown.
    • Your samosa is ready

    Description of samosa snack

    Samosa is a crispy, savory pastry filled with a flavorful mixture of ingredients, typically including potatoes,peas, onions and spices.

    It is usually triangular or cone-shaped, with a crunchy outer layer giving way to a soft, aromatic interior.
